Origami Fox (Beginner-Easy)

Depending on the difficulty level, origami cats can be either very easy to both decorate
and create, or they can be extremely difficult to even finish. So, let's attempt to create one!

Materials:

  • Origami paper
  • A pencil (if you need to mark the folding spots)

Instructions:

  1. Fold your origami paper in half [fold the left corner to the right corner]. Unfold.
  2. Fold the upper left and right sides to the center.
  3. Now fold the bottom left and right sides to the center.
  4. Fold in half by folding the bottom corner to the top.
  5. Fold most [not all] of the flap back down.
  6. Open the pocket on the left side and squash down while folding the left side of the triangle to the center. Do the same to the right side.
  7. Fold the figure in half by folding the right side to the left.
  8. Fold the thin triangle to the right. This is the tail.
  9. Unfold the tail.
  10. Outside reverse fold the tail. Open the figure and bend the tail up along the creases you just made. Close the figure and crease the sides in.
  11. Fold the bottom corner up, make sure the base lines up with the base of the tail. Do the same for the other side.
  12. Open up the body and fold the top.
  13. Fold the flap up and flatten well.
  14. Open the top flap, fold down, and crease the sides. Turn the figure over.
  15. Fold the corners of the "neck." Turn the figure over again.
  16. Push in the middle of the top of the head and crease the front edges.
  17. Fold the bottom corner of the face up to form the nose.
  18. Unfold the feet a little so they're perpendicular with the floor.
  19. Twirl the tail and you're done!
